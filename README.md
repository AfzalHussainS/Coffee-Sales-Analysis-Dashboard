# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, customer ordering behavior, and product trends.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics such as **Total Sales, Total Orders, and Quantity Sold**, along with month-on-month (MoM) comparisons and time-based sales patterns.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
The coffee shop business requires a centralized and visual solution to:
- Track monthly sales performance
- Identify growth or decline in sales, orders, and quantities
- Understand customer purchasing behavior across time, location, and product categories
- Detect peak sales days and hours for better operational planning

---

## 📊 KPI Requirements

### 1. Total Sales Analysis
- Calculate total sales for each month  
- Determine month-on-month (MoM) increase or decrease  
- Calculate the difference between the selected month and the previous month  

### 2. Total Orders Analysis
- Calculate total number of orders per month  
- Identify MoM increase or decrease in orders  
- Compare orders with the previous month  

### 3. Total Quantity Sold Analysis
- Calculate total quantity sold per month  
- Identify MoM increase or decrease  
- Compare quantity sold with the previous month  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visuals & Analysis

### 1. Calendar Heat Map
- Dynamic calendar based on selected month
- Color intensity represents sales volume
- Tooltips display **Sales, Orders, and Quantity** per day

### 2. Weekday vs Weekend Sales Analysis
- Segmentation of weekday and weekend sales
- Helps identify demand patterns and behavioral differences

### 3. Sales by Store Location
- Comparison of sales across different store locations
- Month-on-month difference indicators included

### 4. Daily Sales Trend with Average Line
- Bar chart showing daily sales for the selected month
- Average daily sales line for benchmarking
- Highlights days above and below average performance

### 5. Sales by Product Category
- Analysis of sales contribution by product categories
- Identifies top revenue-generating categories

### 6. Top 10 Products by Sales
- Displays top-performing products based on sales
- Enables quick identification of best-selling items

### 7. Sales by Days and Hours (Heat Map)
- Heat map showing sales distribution by day and hour
- Tooltips provide detailed metrics for each time slot

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI Desktop**
  - Power Query for data cleaning and transformation
  - DAX for calculated measures and KPIs
  - Interactive slicers and tooltips
- **SQL**
  - Used for data preparation and aggregation before visualization
